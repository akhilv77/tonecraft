Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

ToneCraft does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to its own servers. There are no analytics, no user accounts, and no data sales — ever.

1. What data we process

ToneCraft processes the following data locally on your device:

  • Selected text — text you highlight on a webpage. Capped at 20,000 characters and stored temporarily in chrome.storage.local to pass it to the popup. It is never sent anywhere except to the OpenRouter API when you click a tone button.
  • OpenRouter API key — the key you enter during onboarding or in Settings. Stored in chrome.storage.local (on-device only; never synced to Google's servers). It leaves your device only in the Authorization header of requests to https://openrouter.ai/api/v1/chat/completions.
  • Preferences — dark mode setting and chosen AI model. Stored in chrome.storage.sync (non-sensitive; syncs across your Chrome-signed-in devices) and never transmitted to any third-party server.

2. How your selected text is used

When you click Polite, Formal, or Elaborate, the selected text is sent directly from your browser to the OpenRouter API (openrouter.ai) using your own API key. The request goes directly from your browser to OpenRouter — no intermediate server.

ToneCraft applies a 512 KB guard on API responses — any response larger than this is rejected before its content is processed or displayed.

OpenRouter's handling of that data is governed by their own Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing it before use.

3. Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not operate any servers or databases.
  • We do not collect usage analytics, crash reports, or telemetry of any kind.
  • We do not track which websites you visit or what text you select.
  • We do not share any data with third parties (other than the OpenRouter request described above).
  • We do not use cookies or any tracking technology.

4. Permissions used

  • storage — to save your API key, model choice, and dark mode preference locally in your browser.
  • activeTab — to access the current tab when you open the popup via keyboard shortcut or context menu.
  • contextMenus — to add the "Rewrite with ToneCraft" option when you right-click selected text.
  • host_permissions (openrouter.ai) — to allow the popup to call the OpenRouter API directly from your browser.

5. Data retention

Selected text is stored in chrome.storage.local and is overwritten each time you make a new selection. Your API key remains in chrome.storage.local until you clear it in Settings or uninstall the extension. Preferences (model choice, dark mode) remain in chrome.storage.sync until you clear them in Settings or uninstall the extension.

Uninstalling ToneCraft removes all locally stored data immediately.

6. Children's privacy

ToneCraft is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly process data from children.

7. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify users via the Chrome Web Store listing.
